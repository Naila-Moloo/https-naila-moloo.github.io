About Me

Hi! I’m a 19-year-old interested in creating technological solutions to address our global consumption of fossil fuels. I am a Morehead-Cain Scholar at UNC at Chapel Hill studying materials engineering. I also love reading, listening to history podcasts, playing sports, and writing. My debut novel was published when I was 14 and was a #1 bestseller in magical realism. The sequel, "Chronicles of Illusions: Bound by Dreams" was released in May 2022 and is available on all platforms. You can find both books on Amazon here.

What I'm Doing Now

Solar Energy

One of my main projects is a flexible plastic-free solar cell. Leveraging solar’s intersection with nanotechnology, I have been working in a lab at TMU and recently built a small scale working prototype.

Writing

Writing has been a passion of mine for years, and in February 2021 I got my first novel published! The sequel "Bound by Dreams" was released in May 2022. I also enjoy writing articles on Medium.

Bioplastics

I am currently developing a bioplastic from duckweed through a collaboration with Pond Biomaterials, and have been working in a biochemistry lab at Carleton University.

Public Speaking

I have gotten the opportunity to speak across global stages and events including Web Summit, Microsoft Ignite, Zappos All Hands, Reuters IMPACT, Ignite Digital, and COI. I have also given talks and spoken on several panels for companies like The Walrus, Scouts Canada, DMZ, and the David Suzuki Foundation.
